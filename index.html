Welcome to my web page on the results of gaming survey carried out in year 9 in a school.

It's goal is to show clearly the results of a survey I carried out in a school and compare them to those of other similar surveys. My survey focused on the year 9s in the school in particular. All answers were recorded at 31 responders, although more have answered since
